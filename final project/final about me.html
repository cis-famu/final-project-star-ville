MY FAVORITES

Shows

GRAVITY FALLS aired on Disney Channel on June 15, 2012. This animated tv show follows twins DIPPER and MABEL PINES as they spend their summer with their con artist gret-uncle, GRUNKLE STAN, in the mysterious sleepy town of Gravity Falls, Oregon.

SCOOBY-DOO! MYSTERY INCORPORATED is another reboot in the Scooby Doo franchise. It origanally aired on April 5, 2010 on Cartoon Network. Unlikely the other reboots, MYSTERY INCORPORATED is serialized and follows a mystery that is haunting the town of Crystal Cove.

Podcast

Snap Judgement by Snap Judgement and PRX is a podcast that invites guests to retell stories in which they had to make snap judgments. These stories can range from stealing a chocolate bar as a kid to trying to survive. Each episode starts with the host telling a story from his own life to introduce the story the storyteller will tell. Throughout the podcast episode, music is used to build on the emotions of the storyteller, mainly to build suspense. The storytellers speak as if they are in an interview, making it feel as if we, the audience, are asking them the questions.

Bands

PARAMORE is a 3 member rock band that was formed in Franklin, Tennessee in 2004. The current, and my personal favorite, lineup consists of lead vocalist Hayley Williams, guitarist Taylor York and drummer Zac Farro. Their most recent album THIS IS WHY features a more relaxed 70s rock sound. If I were to name my favorites from this album, I would name the entire tracklist.

TOMORROW X TOGETHER is a 5 member korean boyband. They debuted on March 4, 2019 under bighit entertainment with members Soobin, Yeonjun, Beomgyu, Taehyun, and Huening Kai. They recently made a comeback with the title track CHASING THAT FEELING on their third full length album THE NAME CHAPTER: FREEFALL. My personal favorites from this album are GROWING PAIN, DREAMER, DEEP DOWN, and SKIPPING STONES.

THE BACKSEAT LOVERS is a 4 member rock band from Provo, Utah that was formed in 2018. The members include Joshua Harmonn, Jonas Swanson, Juice Welch, and KJ Ward. Their most recent release WAITING TO SPILL came out on October 28, 2022. I discovered THE BACKSEAT LOVERS in mid 2023. Since then, I've had CLOSE YOUR EYES, GROWING/DYING, SLOWING DOWN, and KNOW YOUR NAME (all of which are featured on WAITING TO SPILL) on repeat. I also have WATCH YOUR MOUTH, POOL HOUSE, INTUITION, KILBY GIRL, DAVY CROCHET, and MAPLE SYRUP (all of which are featured on their debut album WHEN WE WERE FRIENDS) on constant rotation.
