Rhythm Rundown

Rhythm Rundown is a music podcast by Jaylen Bain, Allyssa Taffe, and Ondrea Wagnor. In each episode, we invite music lovers to listen as we delve into different topics about music and the music industry.

EPISODES

In this episode, we discuss homecoming. In the very first episode of Rhythm Rundown, we share stories about our homecoming experience this year. Listen to the homecoming experience of new and returning students.

MY RECOMMENDED SONGS

In this episode, we will discuss how streaming has affected music production, consumption, and distribution. We then took a look into sampling. Not only did we look into the history, but also what constitutes as good or bad sampling.

In this episode, we discussed some of the most influential and most forgotten in male-dominated genres: women. We will highlight and discuss the contributions of women in rock and rap. We will also examine the public’s response to women in these male-dominated genres.

This episode discusses three international artists: the Beatles, Bob Marley, and Celia Cruz. The Beatles, one of the biggest boy bands, ushered in the British invasion. Bob Marley presented messages through his music internationally. Celia Cruz dazzled the world as the Queen of salsa.

In this episode, we discuss the role of streaming platforms and the history of vinyl. We primarily focused on Spotify, one of the biggest streaming platforms today. We also discussed the difference between vinyl and CDs. Listen to see if we discover everyone’s new love for vinyl.
